About

Our success story is rooted in Telkomsel's history as a telecommunications and broadband pioneer in Indonesia. Each milestone below is the foundation for the data insights and infrastructure we use to win your digital market.

  • May 26, 1995

    the beginning of Telkomsel's founding

    This journey began when Telkomsel was founded as a joint venture to provide nationwide wireless telecommunications services. Its first product, kartuHalo, became the first postpaid service in Indonesia. This laid the foundation for the network that now forms the backbone of our digital marketing strategy.

  • December 2014

    Accelerating the Broadband Era

    Telkomsel is leading the adoption of mobile broadband in Indonesia with the commercial launch of 4G LTE services. This move fundamentally changes the way consumers access the internet and content.

  • May 2021

    Future Network Pioneers

    Telkomsel became the first operator to officially launch commercial 5G services in Indonesia. This represents a major leap towards ultra-high-speed, low-latency connectivity.

  • Since 2022 - Present

    Transforming into a Digital Lifestyle Enabler

    Telkomsel is undergoing a major transformation, becoming not only a connectivity provider but also a Digital Lifestyle Enabler, integrating Fixed Mobile Convergence (FMC) services through IndiHome.
